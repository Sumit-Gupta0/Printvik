## 🎯 IDEA KYA THA?

Ek complete print business platform banana jisme:
- Customers online se print order kar sake
- Operators print jobs manage kar sake
- Delivery partners deliveries handle kar sake
- Admin pura business manage kar sake

**Simple Words Mein:** Jaise Zomato food delivery ke liye hai, waise hi Printvik printing ke liye!

---

## 👥 PLATFORM KE 4 MAIN USERS

### 1. CUSTOMER (End User)
**Kya Kar Sakta Hai:**
- Mobile/web se print order kar sakta hai
- Documents upload kar sakta hai (PDF, images)
- Instant price dekh sakta hai
- Delivery ya pickup choose kar sakta hai
- Online payment ya COD kar sakta hai
- Order track kar sakta hai
- Order history dekh sakta hai
- Multiple addresses save kar sakta hai

### 2. OPERATOR (Print Shop Owner)
**Kya Kar Sakta Hai:**
- Apne print jobs dekh sakta hai
- Job status update kar sakta hai (printing, printed, ready)
- Apni earnings dekh sakta hai
- Daily/weekly/monthly reports dekh sakta hai
- Quality check kar sakta hai

### 3. DELIVERY PARTNER
**Kya Kar Sakta Hai:**
- Assigned deliveries dekh sakta hai
- Delivery status update kar sakta hai
- Pickup aur delivery addresses dekh sakta hai
- COD collect kar sakta hai
- Delivery proof upload kar sakta hai
- Apni earnings track kar sakta hai

### 4. ADMIN (Business Owner)
**Kya Kar Sakta Hai:**
- Saare orders dekh sakta hai
- Operators ko jobs assign kar sakta hai
- Delivery partners ko assign kar sakta hai
- Users manage kar sakta hai
- Analytics dekh sakta hai (revenue, orders, etc.)
- Pricing control kar sakta hai
- Business reports dekh sakta hai

## 📱 USER FLOWS

### Customer Flow:
1. Register/Login
2. Upload documents
3. Select specifications
4. Check delivery availability
5. Choose payment method
6. Place order
7. Receive confirmation email
8. Track order
9. Receive updates via email
10. Get delivery

### Operator Flow:
1. Login
2. View print queue
3. Start printing
4. Mark as printed
5. Quality check
6. Mark as ready
7. Track earnings

### Delivery Flow:
1. Login
2. View assigned deliveries
3. Pick up from operator
4. Update status to in-transit
5. Deliver to customer
6. Collect COD (if applicable)
7. Mark as delivered
8. Upload proof

### Admin Flow:
1. Login
2. View all orders
3. Assign to operator
4. Monitor progress
5. Assign to delivery
6. Track analytics
7. Manage users
8. View reports
